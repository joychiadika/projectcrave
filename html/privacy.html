Privacy and Security Policy for Crave

Effective Date:

Welcome to Crave, the innovative culinary project designed to cater to a wide range of users with diverse culinary needs. This Privacy and Security Policy outlines how we collect, use, protect, and manage your personal information and data. By using Crave, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

  1. Personal Information:

    When you create an account, we may collect your name, email address, and password. If you choose to connect through social media, we may collect information from your social media profile. We may collect payment information if you make purchases within the app.

  2. Usage Information:

    We collect data about your interactions with the app, including recipes viewed, cooking methods used, and dietary preferences.

  3. Device Information:

    We may collect information about the device you use to access Crave, such as device type, operating system, and unique identifiers.

How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  1. Personalization: To tailor the app to your culinary preferences and dietary restrictions.
  2. Improvement: To enhance the user experience, develop new features, and provide relevant content.
  3. Communication: To send you updates, notifications, and relevant information.
  4. Account Management: To manage your account, including password resets and account recovery.
  5. Analytics: To analyze app usage and trends to improve our services.

Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously and employ industry-standard measures to protect it:

  1. Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted to safeguard your privacy.

  2. Access Control: We restrict access to your information to authorized personnel only.

  3. Regular Audits: We conduct security audits and assessments to identify and mitigate potential risks.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party service providers who help us deliver our services, subject to strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies and tracking technologies to improve user experience and gather analytics. You can manage your cookie preferences in your device settings.

Your Choices

  • Account Deletion: You can delete your Crave account at any time.
  • Data Access: You can request access to the data we hold about you.
  • Marketing Communications: You can opt-out of marketing communications.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy and Security Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the effective date will be updated accordingly.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding your privacy or security, please contact us at [contact@email.com]. Thank you for using Crave. We are committed to ensuring that your culinary journey with us is both delightful and secure.
